About Prasad Hotel

Our Story

Founded in 1995, Prasad Hotel began as a small eatery with a big dream: to share the joy of pure, sattvic vegetarian food. Our founder, Mrs. Ananda Prasad, believed that food should not only be delicious but also nourish the body and soul.

Today, we continue that tradition. Every dish we serve is a testament to our commitment to quality, hygiene, and authentic flavors. We are more than just a restaurant; we are a family that welcomes you to experience the true taste of vegetarianism.
